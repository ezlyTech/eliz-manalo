About Me

I'm an aspiring website developer based in Cavite, Philippines. I'm 18 years old, a computer science student at the same time working as a digital designer.


You see, I've always been fascinated by how things work and how things are put together—and that fascination has only grown as I've gotten older. In college, I'm currently studying computer science, which gave me the opportunity to finally start building things for myself.


When I started working as a digital designer, it was exciting for me to be able to apply my technical knowledge to create something that other people could use and enjoy. And now? Now that I've built up a solid foundation and experience in design, I'm excited to start diving into the world of web development—and I can't wait to see what else I can learn!
